About SC DECA What is DECA?

Business class shouldn't feel theoretical.

South Carolina DECA has prepared emerging leaders and entrepreneurs in marketing, finance, hospitality, and management since 1943. Today, more than 50 chapters and 3,000+ members compete, network, and build real skills — backed by a curriculum that ties directly into the classroom, not around it.

Since 1943

83 Years of Building Leaders

South Carolina DECA prepares emerging leaders and entrepreneurs in marketing, finance, hospitality, and management — and has since 1943.

Mission

South Carolina DECA organizes competitive events and leadership initiatives that uphold our values of competency, teamwork, integrity, and innovation.

Vision

South Carolina DECA motivates young leaders to find success in their passion within the business world.
